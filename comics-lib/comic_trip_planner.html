Trip Planner

Los Angeles Comic-con

The city of angels holds a huge comic-con annually, and where better than to go right where all the action is! Surrounded by major studios, corporations, A-list stars, and amusement parks, the Los Angeles experience is one sure to steal the show.

New York Comic-con

Prepare for a marvelous visit when you go to New York CIty! This bustling metropolis is the iconic headquarters of MARVEL comics and infamous in the realm of story-telling. The New York comic-con is a massive gathering of fans from all over in a great big melting pot. In the Big Apple, you’re sure to find something new and extravagant to take back with you.

Tokyo Expo

Venture to the archipelago of Japan and behold the capital of Tokyo! This ginormous futuristic city hosts the Tokyo Comic-con, a huge combination of American and Japanese entertainment all jam-packed into a single weekend! This trip is sure to be full of exotic souvenirs and cosplayers that you won’t find anywhere else!
